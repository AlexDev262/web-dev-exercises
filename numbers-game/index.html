Numbers Game

Rules

You have $1000, and can choose how much you'll bet.

You have 2 betting options:

  • Select a multiplier - to multiply your bet by. A number will be rolled (0-100) and if it's less than your chance, you win.
  • Choose a lucky number (from 0 to 100), and bet if the rolled number would be <, > or = to your number.

    • If you get lucky, the number rolls in your favour, and your bet gets multiplied.

    Then, you get the option to cash out, or continue betting with the money you've won.

    Good luck!
