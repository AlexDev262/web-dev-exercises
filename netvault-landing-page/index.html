Start tracking your time! Time is money.

The Timer

Use the timer to lock in your worked hours. Each time you sit down to work, set the duration on the timer and click start. Once it's finished, you get notified, and the work block well get logged onto the calendar immediatly!

Calendar & Events

Schedule your events on our calendar with ease. You can also sync your existing schedule from google calendar here. Get notified when an event is starting. Use our template for repeating schedule. The timer will auto set its duration based on your schedule!

Activities and FPI

You can add activities that might be recurring in your schedule. You can set a certain FPI - Future Potential Income score for each activity based on it's intrinsic value. FPI is your reward for successfully finishing an activity!

Challenges

Challenge yourself to finish a certain ammount of hours for a job you need! Once you finish the hours, your challenge will be marked completed, and you'll recieve a difficulty badge for each challenge based on the completed hours (common, rare, elite and hardcore)!
